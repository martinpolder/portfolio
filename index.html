About Me

Academics

My name is Martin Polder, I am an aspiring web developer from sunny (& humid) Orlando, Florida. I previously attended Florida State University where I studied History and Environmental science, making the Dean's List and volunteering weekly for environmental service projects.

After acquiring my Associate in Arts degree from Florida State, I left the university to explore other avenues and found the UCF coding boot camp. The full stack web developer course was a perfect match and transformed me into a compitent, competitive coder.


Creativity & Leisure

When I'm not coding, I enjoy playing guitar and composing music, riding my trusty bicycle all over the state, and supporting the two greatest teams on earth: the Philadelphia Eagles (Super Bowl 52 Champions), and Liverpool FC (6-Times European Champions). I also love to play fetch and cuddle with my adorable little black manx cat, Dakota.
